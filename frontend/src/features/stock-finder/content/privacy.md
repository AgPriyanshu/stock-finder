# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

## 1. What We Collect

- **Phone number** — used as your account identity for login and contact.
- **Location** — GPS coordinates or the map pin you place when registering a shop; used to show nearby results to buyers.
- **Search queries** — logged anonymously to improve result quality and detect abuse.
- **Error and usage data** — pages visited and errors encountered, sent to Sentry with personally identifiable information removed.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- **Matchmaking** — we use location to surface relevant nearby inventory and shops.
- **Fraud prevention** — phone numbers and activity patterns help us detect and block abusive accounts.
- **Service improvement** — aggregated, anonymised search logs help us tune relevance.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

## 3. Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active.
- Search logs are retained for 90 days.
- If you delete your account, your personal data is removed within 30 days, except where required by applicable law.

## 4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser `localStorage` to store your login token and remember your cookie consent preference. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

## 5. Data Deletion Requests

To request deletion of your account and associated data, email **privacy@atlas-platform.example** with the subject line **"Data Deletion Request"** and your registered phone number. We will process requests within 30 days.

## 6. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via the app.

## 8. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact **privacy@atlas-platform.example**.
